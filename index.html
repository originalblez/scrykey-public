Why your own keys?

Most AI search apps charge subscriptions and keep your query history on their servers. Scrykey does neither. You bring two API keys and keep full control.

No tracking, no profiling

Queries go directly from your device to Brave and Mistral. There is no Scrykey server in between.

No ads, ever

You pay the provider directly at cost. No markup, no sponsored results, no dark patterns.

Transparent pricing

Brave gives 1,000 free searches per month. Mistral costs about $0.50 for 1,000 searches. You can check your usage anytime in the app.

Get started in two minutes

  1. 1

    Get a Brave Search key

    Sign up at brave.com/search/api. You get 1,000 free searches per month, then $5 per 1,000 after that.

  2. 2

    Get a Mistral AI key

    Sign up at console.mistral.ai. The small model Scrykey uses costs fractions of a cent per query.

  3. 3

    Paste them into Scrykey

    Keys are stored in your system Keychain. They never touch a Scrykey server — because there isn’t one.

Why Brave and Mistral

Brave Search runs its own independent web index — not a front-end for Bing or Google — so your queries aren’t funnelled back to the companies Scrykey exists to avoid.

Mistral AI is a European provider with a clear data-handling stance. Its small model is fast, cheap, and genuinely good at the “read these results, answer with citations” task Scrykey uses it for.

Bring your own keys

No subscription, no middleman. You own the accounts, the billing, and the data relationship with each provider.

On-device only

There is no Scrykey server. Queries and keys never leave your device except to go to the providers you configured.
