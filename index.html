Why Brave and Mistral

Most search apps hand your queries to Google, Bing, or a frontier AI lab that trains on your data. Scrykey doesn’t.

Brave Search runs its own independent web index. It isn’t a front-end for Bing or Google, so your queries aren’t funnelled back to the companies Scrykey exists to avoid.

Mistral AI is a European model provider with a clearer data-handling stance than most US frontier labs. Its small model is genuinely good at the “read these search results, answer with citations” task Scrykey uses it for.

What 1,000 searches per month costs

Brave includes $5 of free monthly credits, which covers around 1,000 calls at the current rate. Mistral is billed in fractions of a cent per search — roughly $0.50 for 1,000 basic searches on mistral-small-latest.

So: a heavy month of a thousand searches costs about $0.50. A light month of a hundred searches costs nothing. You pay the providers directly — there is no Scrykey middleman and no Scrykey bill.

On-device only

Your queries go directly from your device to the providers whose API keys you configure. There is no Scrykey server. Keys live in the system Keychain.
