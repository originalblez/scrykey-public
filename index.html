How it works

  1. 1

    Get two API keys

    Sign up with Brave Search and Mistral. Brave includes free monthly credits; Mistral’s small model is cheap enough that typical use costs pennies.

  2. 2

    Paste them into Scrykey

    Keys live in the system Keychain on your device. They never touch a Scrykey server — because there isn’t one.

  3. 3

    Search

    Queries go directly from your device to the providers you configured. You pay them, not us.

Why Brave and Mistral

Most search apps hand your queries to Google, Bing, or a frontier lab that trains on your data. Scrykey doesn’t.

Brave Search runs its own independent web index — not a front-end for Bing or Google — so queries aren’t funnelled back to the companies Scrykey exists to avoid.

Mistral AI is a European provider with a clearer data-handling stance than most US frontier labs, and its small model is genuinely good at the “read these results, answer with citations” task Scrykey uses it for.

Bring your own keys

No subscription, no middleman. You own the accounts, you own the billing, you own the data relationship with each provider.

On-device only

There is no Scrykey server. Queries and keys never leave your device except to go to the providers you configured.
